Sourdough timing

The plan

Mix at 8am, fold every 30 min until 11am, shape, cold retard overnight. Bake at 9am the next day. Adjusts in summer — start later, cut the bulk by an hour.

What to remember

  • Levain at 1:5:5, ready when it doubles
  • Dough temp target: 25°C — adjust water
  • Score with the blade flat, not vertical
